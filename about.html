What we are

Established in January 2020, we are known for organizing the most creative and unique events in Lovely Professional Univeristy. Where there is hope, there's Ignite.

01.Our Mission

Every student is a prodigy and their talents must be addressed. At Ignite, we provide various levels of training and experience to help people achieve their goals both individually and collectively as a group.

02.Vission

We aim at giving stage to every individual so that they could polish their talents and prove their mettle to the world.

03.Our Connections

Network is the networth. At Ignite, we believe connections and collaborations are the two most important things in the professional world. We have connections both in and out university ranging from big MNCs to NGOs.
